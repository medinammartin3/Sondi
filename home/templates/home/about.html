About Us

Welcome to Sondi, a free platform for creating and participating in online polls! Sondi is a project born out of a passion for web development and a love for creating interactive, user-friendly applications.

From your profile, you can:

  • Create: Design polls with multiple options, having the choice between make them private or public.
  • Vote: Participate in polls shared with you or created by others and see real-time results.
  • Share: Share your polls with friends, colleagues, or the whole world, just by sharing your poll's code.
  • Manage: Keep track of all your polls in one place, view results, and close polls when they're done.

This project is a summer 2024 initiative, aimed at learning and mastering Django, a powerful web framework for Python. Through Sondi, I've explored the depths of Django's capabilities, from database management to user authentication, all while building a tool that makes it easy for anyone who wants to create and share polls for free.

My goal was to provide a simple yet powerful platform where users can voice their opinions and make decisions collaboratively. Sondi is designed to make the process as seamless and enjoyable as possible.

Thank you for joining Sondi!
